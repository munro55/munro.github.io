Noob Inc Button Script

A Google Sheets automation tool that makes your spreadsheets smarter with custom button controls.

What it does

Noob Inc Button Script connects to your Google Sheets to add custom interactive buttons and automation. It helps you trigger actions, run scripts, and manage your spreadsheet data more efficiently without writing complex formulas.

Data & Permissions

This app requests access to your Google Sheets data solely to perform the automation tasks you set up. It does not collect, store, or share your personal information with any third parties.
